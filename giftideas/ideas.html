Sustainable Christmas gifts

Surprise your clients and employees with a Adoptrees tree — an impactful, eco-friendly gesture that’s just a click away.

Gift trees, create a connection

A simple yet meaningful digital gift. Plant a tree and share the experience with your employees or clients. Through our Adoptrees platform, recipients can learn all about their tree and explore the story behind the agroforestry project. Each tree can even be named after the recipient, making the experience even more intimate and personal. Over 10,000 companies worldwide have already chosen us, and our community of tree keepers counts over one million people.

Be part of a global movement. Plant a tree today, and give a gift that grows with time.
